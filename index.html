Contact Customer Support

View in Help Scout

We'd love to help you learn about mindfulness meditation using all the Ten Percent Happier resources and look forward to hearing from you.

How to Contact Us

  • Email us at support@tenpercent.com.
  • Send us a message through our website contact form.
  • Best for troubleshooting the app: If you're signed into the Ten Percent Happier app on your iPhone, iPad or Android mobile, the best way to contact us is through the settings. When you contact us this way the app automatically includes some information that might help us solve your problem. Here's how to send that email:
    • Open your Profile in the Ten Percent Happier app.
      • Apple: Tap the Profile icon in the upper, left corner of the screen.
      • Android: Tap the Profile icon in the upper, right corner of the screen.
    • Tap Settings (gear icon) in the upper, right corner.
    • Scroll down and tap Contact a Human under SUPPORT. If an email doesn't open up automatically, you'll need to sign into the email app on your mobile device. This might be Apple Mail, Gmail, Yahoo Mail or some other mail app.
    • Send us the email.

We don't currently have a phone support system.
